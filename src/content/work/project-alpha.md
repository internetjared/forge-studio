Fenwick & Co. had been quietly excellent for three decades. The problem: nobody outside their existing client base knew it. Their previous site was built in 2017, loaded in 8 seconds, and communicated nothing distinctive about why they were worth hiring over any other consulting firm.

## The Challenge

The brief was clear: we needed to take everything that made Fenwick exceptional — their proprietary methodology, their 98% client retention rate, their contrarian takes on industry orthodoxy — and make it legible to a digital audience that would give them maybe seven seconds before navigating away.

The secondary challenge was internal. The partners were proud of what they'd built. The rebrand had to feel like an evolution, not a repudiation.

## The Approach

We started with a three-week discovery phase: stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and a full audit of their existing materials. What emerged was clear — Fenwick's differentiator was their willingness to tell clients uncomfortable truths. Every competitor in the space positioned around "partnership" and "alignment." Fenwick positioned around results, even when that meant short-term friction.

**Positioning platform**: "The consultants who tell you what you need to hear."

**Visual language**: Dark, authoritative, typographically heavy. Clash Display for headlines, General Sans for body. A near-black background that immediately signals this is not your typical consulting firm.

**Site architecture**: Five pages, nothing more. Homepage that establishes authority immediately, work page with real results (not vague case studies), services page that explains their methodology in plain English, about page that humanizes the partners, contact page that respects the prospect's time.

## The Result

The site launched on time and on budget. Inbound leads tripled in the first quarter. The partners stopped having to explain why Fenwick was different — the site did it for them.
